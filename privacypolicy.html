Privacy Policy

JAPA Sadhna Counter

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to JAPA Sadhna Counter. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app stores, how it is handled, and how you can delete it at any time.

2. Data We Store (Local Only)

JAPA Sadhna Counter stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Japa count — the number of mantra repetitions you complete.
  • Mala count — the number of malas (rounds) you complete.

This data is saved locally on your device only, used solely to display your personal spiritual practice progress. It is never uploaded to any server, cloud, or external service.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

⚠️ Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance:

  • Advertising ID — A unique, user-resettable identifier used for ad personalization and frequency capping.
  • App interactions — Ad clicks, impressions, and other interactions with ads.
  • Device information — Device model, OS version, and network type (for ad delivery).

How Google uses this data: Google uses this information to:

  • Show you relevant ads (personalized or non-personalized based on your device settings)
  • Prevent duplicate ad views and click fraud
  • Measure ad performance and improve ad quality

User Choice: You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time by going to Settings → Google → Ads on your Android device.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service in our app:

  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements. AdMob is a Google service that collects data as described above. Google processes this data in accordance with its privacy policies.

No other third-party services (analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, etc.) are used.

5. Health & Wellness Clarification

JAPA Sadhna Counter is a spiritual practice counter app. While regular japa and meditation practice may naturally support general mental and spiritual well-being, this app does not collect, access, read, or write any health or medical data of any kind.

Specifically, the app does not:

  • Access or connect to Google Fit, Apple Health, or any health platform.
  • Track any physical health metrics such as heart rate, steps, sleep, or calories.
  • Collect or store any medical, biometric, or sensitive personal information.
  • Use your japa or mala counts for any health-related profiling or analysis.

6. Local Storage & Data Control

Your japa count and mala count are stored using your device's local storage. This means:

  • The data exists only on your device.
  • We have no access to it whatsoever.
  • No third party has access to it (except AdMob's advertising ID as described above).
  • It is not backed up to any cloud by this app.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

How long is data kept?

  • Japa/Mala counts: Retained locally on your device for as long as the app remains installed.
  • Advertising ID data: Managed by Google. You can reset your Advertising ID anytime via device settings.

How to delete your data: You have full control over your data at all times. You can permanently delete it using any of the following methods:

  • In-app reset: Use the reset or clear option in the app's settings to instantly wipe all saved counts and history.
  • Device settings: Go to Settings → Apps → JAPA Sadhna Counter → Storage → Clear Data.
  • Uninstall the app: Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.

8. Internet Permissions

The app uses internet permission solely for displaying advertisements via Google AdMob. No personal or sensitive data is transmitted to us or any other third party beyond what AdMob collects as described above.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.
