What is Japa?

Japa is the repetition of sacred mantras used in meditation and spiritual growth. Traditionally done using a mala of 108 beads, this app allows you to perform japa digitally anytime and anywhere with ease.

Why Choose This App?

This Japa Counter App helps you stay consistent in your daily spiritual routine. Whether you chant "Om Namah Shivaya", "Hare Krishna" or any mantra, this app makes your sadhna simple, focused and accessible.
